Bin Buddy

An Alexa skill for finding domestic waste collection dates in Wiltshire

GENERAL INFORMATION

This skill works only for the Wiltshire local authority.

This skill requires permission to access your address. Your address is required to search the Wiltshire authority's public bin date service for finding bin collection dates. The skill cannot work without an address and postcode.

This skill is free to use. It is not provided by the Wiltshire authority and has been developed independently.

Should the authority alter it bin collection website this skill may not work.

PRIVACY NOTICE

No data is not stored or retained by this skill whatsoever.
